Why Report?

Your reports help healthcare professionals and regulatory authorities identify new side effects and take appropriate action to minimize risks. Even if you're unsure if a symptom is related to a medication, reporting it can help establish patterns.

Experiencing unexpected effects from a medication? Report it here to help others. Your information is kept confidential and will be used to improve medication safety.

MediSafe

MediSafe is a user-friendly platform for healthcare providers and patients to easily report adverse drug reactions. Our mission is to improve safety and healthcare through real-time reporting and monitoring of medication side effects.
